Project Overview

Lead Hygiene

Automated cleaning and structuring of inbound data from waitlists, forms, and emails into centralized tracking tools.

AI Qualification

Utilizes LLMs to tag lead intent, determine priority levels (P1-P3), and summarize pain points instantly.

Auto-Drafting

Generates context-aware, personalized follow-up drafts based on proven sales scripts and incoming lead data.

Who Is This For?

Early-Stage SaaS

Founders and Growth Heads who need to validate sales motions without hiring a full-scale sales team immediately.

Solo SDRs

Execution-heavy roles requiring tools to manage massive inbound volume while maintaining "human-touch" personalization.

Implementation Strategy

The Serverless Bridge Approach

This project leverages a serverless architecture to ensure zero maintenance and maximum scalability for growing SaaS startups.

  • Data Ingestion: Capturing Webhooks from WooCommerce and Google Forms.
  • Logic Layer: Google Apps Script acts as the orchestrator, handling API calls to HubSpot and OpenAI.
  • Intelligence: GPT-4o integration analyzes unstructured text to extract "Intent Tags" (High/Medium/Low).
  • CRM Integration: Direct injection into HubSpot's CRM V3 API for seamless hand-off to Account Executives.
